# Student User Guide

This guide explains how students use the kiosk app for day-to-day inventory tasks.

## 1. What This Guide Covers

Use this guide if you need to:
- Sign in to the kiosk
- Find items you are allowed to use
- Check items out
- Return items
- Submit an item request
- Understand common error messages

## 2. Where To Access The App

Student workflows run from the kiosk path:
- `/kiosk/`

If your school shares direct links, open the student kiosk link provided by your teacher.

## 3. First-Time Sign-In

1. Go to the login screen.
2. Scan your user barcode into the "Waiting for Barcode Scan..." field.
3. Wait for the dashboard to load.

If sign-in fails:
- Too many attempts can trigger a temporary lockout: "Too many login attempts. Locked for 60 seconds."
- Suspended accounts show: "Your account is suspended. Please contact a teacher."

Tip:
- From login, use "Need Help / Request Credential?" if you need access support.

## 4. Understand The Main Navigation

After sign-in, use the sidebar:
- `Dashboard`: your summary and recent activity
- `Inventory`: search and select items
- `Projects`: project-related item usage
- `Operations Hub`: request/operations views your school has enabled
- `Sign Out`: end your session

Your view is permission-based, so some options may be hidden.

## 5. Find Items You Can Use

1. Open `Inventory`.
2. Use the search box to find an item by name, SKU, or category.
3. Review stock and item details.

Important visibility rules:
- You only see items your class level and visibility tags allow.
- If blocked, you may see: "Your class level cannot access this item."

## 6. Check Out Items (Basket Workflow)

1. In `Inventory`, add needed items to your basket.
2. Open the basket (`Basket`).
3. Adjust quantities.
4. Choose checkout destination:
- `My Items (Personal)` for personal checkout
- A project if your account can join/use projects
5. Select `Bulk Checkout` to complete sign-out.

Common checks:
- If you cannot sign out: "You do not have permission to sign out items."
- If stock is unavailable: "Item is out of stock." or "Cannot exceed available stock."

## 7. View What You Currently Have Out

Use your personal/project views to monitor active checkouts:
- `My Items (Personal)` entries represent your personal sign-outs
- Due and overdue states are shown in the app

Use these views regularly to avoid overdue returns.

## 8. Return Items

Return items from your active items/project views using the return/sign-in action for that row.

Best practice:
- Return items as soon as your work is done.
- Verify the item disappears from your active list after return.

## 9. Request An Item

If enabled by your school:

1. Go to `Inventory`.
2. Select `Request Item`.
3. Submit the request details.
4. Track status from the operations/requests area (if visible in your account).

## 10. Session And Safety Notes

- Kiosk sessions can auto-log out after inactivity.
- Opening the basket pauses inactivity countdown while you finish checkout.
- Always use `Sign Out` when done.

## 11. Common Problems

### I cannot sign in
- Check that you scanned the correct barcode.
- Wait 60 seconds if you hit login lockout.
- Contact a teacher if your account is suspended.

### I cannot see an item another student can see
- Class level or visibility tags may differ.
- Ask your teacher to confirm your class assignment.

### Checkout button is disabled
- Add at least one item to the basket.
- Confirm item quantities are valid.

### I submitted a request but do not see updates
- Not all schools enable full student order/request views.
- Ask your teacher to confirm request status.

## 12. Quick Checklist

Before class ends:
- Confirm all required checkouts are complete
- Confirm due times for active items
- Return items no longer needed
- Sign out of the kiosk
